Price Predicting in King County

Using one year of sales data to predict prices

This was an initial look into real estate sales data from one year from King County, Washington, and the goal was to use the given data to make a price predictor.

Our group worked to make a model that not only reduced the R-squared value, but also reduced the root mean squared error (RMSE). Our time limit was 4 days.

We ended up getting a model with a RMSE of ~$130k, but finding out that our next steps would be to obtain even more data to improve the model, as we found out that the regression model was still underfitting the data. True to an iterative approach to data science, we will apply our learnings to future work and this description and file will be updated should an improvement be devised.
